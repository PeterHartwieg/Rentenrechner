## What to build

Apply the locked crawler policy at the Cloudflare Pages level: managed robots / Content Signals set to `search=yes, ai-input=yes, ai-train=no`. Verify that the search and AI-answer crawlers from the locked policy can reach `rentenwiki.de` and that training crawlers are blocked. Document the policy under `docs/seo/`.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Cloudflare zone settings or Pages settings reflect Content Signals: `search=yes, ai-input=yes, ai-train=no`.
- [ ] `dist/robots.txt` (generated by issue 02) explicitly lists allow rules for Googlebot, Bingbot, Applebot, OAI-SearchBot, ChatGPT-User, PerplexityBot.
- [ ] `dist/robots.txt` explicitly lists disallow rules for GPTBot, ClaudeBot, anthropic-ai, Google-Extended, CCBot.
- [ ] Manual verification that representative search/answer bots can reach the homepage and at least two topic pages without WAF/CDN blocks (curl with the bot user-agent against the public domain).
- [ ] Manual verification that training-bot user-agents receive a blocking response or a robots-disallow hit.
- [ ] Policy and verification steps documented at `docs/seo/crawler-policy.md` so future changes have a checklist.
- [ ] No regression to non-bot user traffic (existing rentenwiki.de behavior unchanged).
- [ ] Cloudflare configuration change is recorded in the docs (which dashboard setting, what value) so it survives operator handover.

The locked-policy intent is achieved by composition of two layers:

1. **Cloudflare CDN layer** — `is_robots_txt_managed: true` prepends the Content Signals preamble + a managed disallow block (Amazonbot, Applebot-Extended, Bytespider, CCBot, ClaudeBot, CloudflareBrowserRenderingCrawler, Google-Extended, GPTBot, meta-externalagent) to the served `robots.txt`. The `Content-Signal: search=yes,ai-train=no` header is set. `ai_bots_protection: "block"` enforces at request level for known training bots.

2. **Project layer** — `dist/robots.txt` (generated by `src/seo/robots.ts` from `ALLOWED_BOTS` / `DISALLOWED_BOTS`) is appended after Cloudflare's managed block. This carries the explicit Allow rules for Googlebot, Bingbot, Applebot, OAI-SearchBot, ChatGPT-User, PerplexityBot, plus belt-and-suspenders Disallow lines for the same training bots Cloudflare blocks.

Result: composed `robots.txt` is ~2.1KB, both layers visible in the live response.

`crawler_protection` left disabled — enabling it activates aggressive bot challenges that risk false positives on legitimate users (shared/VPN/Tor exit nodes). Documented in `docs/seo/crawler-policy.md`.

`cf_robots_variant: "off"` left as-is — current variant produces the desired Content Signals output. Other variants would require dashboard exploration before changing.
