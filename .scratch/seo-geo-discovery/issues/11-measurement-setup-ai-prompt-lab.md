## What to build

Documentation-only: capture the steps to set up Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools (including AI Performance), IndexNow, and the manual AI prompt-lab template. **No frontend analytics, no telemetry, no cookies** — measurement remains owner-side only. The prompt-lab template defines a repeatable manual check that records prompts, citation status, cited URL, accuracy, and language-respects-not-advice.
